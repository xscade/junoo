## Steps to Create WhatsApp Business Account

### Step 1: Click "Create account"
- Click the blue **"Create account"** button on the page

### Step 2: Follow the Setup Wizard
You'll be guided through:
1. **Account Details:**
   - Business name (you can use "Xscade" or your business name)
   - Business description
   - Category

2. **Phone Number Linking:**
   - Link your existing phone number (+91 93903 92255)
   - Or add it if not already linked

3. **Verification:**
   - Complete any required verification steps

### Step 3: Complete Phone Number Registration
After creating the account:
- You may need to complete **Phone Number Registration**
- This links your phone number to the business account
- Follow the prompts to complete this

---

## After Creating the Account

Once the account is created:

1. **Your phone number will be fully activated**
2. **Phone Number ID will become available**
3. **You can access it from:**
   - WhatsApp > API Setup
   - WhatsApp Manager > Phone Numbers
